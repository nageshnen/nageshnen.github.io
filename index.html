Full-stack engineer with an applied ML edge

I'm a software engineer and ML practitioner with a Master's in Computer Science from the University of Georgia. At The Home Depot, I build data pipelines and AI-driven tooling that turn complex business problems into reliable, production-grade systems — from Tableau dashboards built on GCP data to proof-of-concept AI agents using Google's Agent Development Kit.

My background spans full-stack development with Django, Flask, Spring, and REST APIs, alongside applied machine learning with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and computer vision tools like OpenCV and YOLO. I've worked hands-on across AWS, GCP, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines, and I care most about building things that are accurate, transparent, and genuinely useful to the people relying on them.
